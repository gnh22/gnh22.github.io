Hi, I'm Grace.

I'm a senior at the University of Pittsburgh studying Computer Science with a minor in Economics.

I first started coding when I was 15 in my high school's AP Computer Science Principles course, and I fell in love with the creative freedom I saw in it. At my core, I'm just someone who enjoys learning new things for my little projects.

Over the years, this desire has manifested itself in sewing, embroidering, crocheting, and I'm using this website as a reminder of the creative power that can be found in Computer Science as well.
